What Is Acai?

Acai is a vibrant, nutrient-rich berry harvested from the acai palm trees native to the Amazon rainforests. Praised for its health benefits, acai is packed with antioxidants, fiber, and heart-healthy fats, making it a superfood favorite. It has a unique, slightly tart flavor and deep purple color. Our menu features this exotic berry in its purest form!
